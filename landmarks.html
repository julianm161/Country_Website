Bueaty of Portugal

For our first landmark, it is Mosteiro dos Jerónimos. It was built in the 16th century to honor Vasco da Gama’s discovery of the sea route to India. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

The second landmark is located in Lisbon, Belém Tower is one of Portugal’s most iconic monuments. Built in the 16th century as a fortress to protect the city. The Belem tower also is a UNESCO World Heritage site

Last but not least, we have the Cathedral of Évora.This stunning cathedral in the historic city of Évora dates back to the 12th century and is a mix of Romanesque, Gothic, and Baroque styles. It is the only landmark on this list which isn't UNESCO World Heritage site
